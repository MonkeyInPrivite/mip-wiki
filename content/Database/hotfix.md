## Method 1 (reccomended)
1. Download the [latest hotfix](https://github.com/KindleModding/Hotfix/releases/latest/download/Update_hotfix_universal.bin)
2. Copy `Update_hotfix_universal.bin` into your `mrpackages` folder
3. Enter `;log mrpi` in the search bar and hit enter **OR** in KUAL go into "Helper" then click "Install MRPackages".

## Method 2 (if MRPI doesn't work)
1. Download the [latest hotfix](https://github.com/KindleModding/Hotfix/releases/latest/download/Update_hotfix_universal.bin).
2. Enable Airplane mode.
3. Restore OTA binaries in KUAL (or whichever other method you used).
4. Copy `Update_hotfix_universal.bin` into your Kindle's root directory.
5. Go into settings and in the 3 dots menu, click "Update Your Kindle".
6. Rename OTA binaries in KUAL (or whichever other method you used)
7. Disable Airplane mode (or don't)
