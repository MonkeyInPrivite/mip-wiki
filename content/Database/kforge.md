Yep, There's a appstore for your jailbroken Kindle, and it has everything from KUAL, KOReader, an [OTA checker](../otastatus/), [Alpine Linux](../alpine/), and more!

Method 1 - From GitHub
Get the [latest release](https://github.com/KindleTweaks/KindleForge/releases/latest/download/KindleForge.zip)
Extract the Zip file
Copy the KindleForge folder and KindleForge.sh into your kindle's documents folder
