The MIP Wiki is ment to be used as an educational tool that is open to the public. That being said, you may showcase this wiki in your content as long as you follow these parameters.

## Paramaters
- Consistent with the proprietary license this project is bound under, you must ask permission from the Owner of the project, or whoever wrote the content that you will be showcasing.
- Before asking permission, please check if your name is on the [allowed licensors](../ALLOWED_LICENSORS.txt) list, in which case you are allowed to use the content on this page that is specified next to your name.
- You cannot promote dangerous/malicious activity towards the site/project in your content, examples include but are not limited to: Encouraging your users to "DDoS" the website or submit malicious commits.
